Topic

We hope that the development of Cormas will continue in a collective way. To share any kind of information about the Cormas tool, a particular Cormas model, or about Agent-Based Simulation in general, please use the Cormas mailing list. It is the right place to report about :

  • Cormas and any associated scientific endeavors
  • Informatics (bugs, suggestions for modifications, addition of new features, etc.)
  • Methodology (the process of modelling),
  • Seminar announcements, calls for papers, job positions, etc.,
  • Bibliographic information.
