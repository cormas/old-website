By plane ...   

Airport Transfers : Montpellier-Méditerranée International Airport is 15 min. from the town centre and 15 km from the CIRAD.

* 14 daily return flights to Paris-Roissy Charles de Gaulle (CDG) and Paris-Orly-Ouest (ORY) - 1 h 10 min. flight time.
* 1 daily return flight from London (Gatwick) - 1 h 30 min. flight time.
* Direct connections to Ajaccio, Bastia, Bordeaux, Lille, Lyon, Nantes, Nice, Perpignan, Rennes, Strasbourg ..
* Direct international connections to Copenhagen, London, Madrid, Rome, Vienne...

Information available on Air France website Airport


By train ...

The SNCF railway station is located in the town centre. 10 daily links to Paris - 3 h 15 min. by TGV (High Speed Train).
* direct SNCF links with Barcelona, Geneva and Milan.
* direct TGV links to Montpellier from the airports of Paris-Roissy-Charles de Gaulle (CDG), Lille and Lyon-Saint Exupéry.

Information available on SNCF website

By car ...

Montpellier is served by the A9 motorway "La Languedocienne ".
The best exit from the A9 to CIRAD is exit n°28 Vendargues, Hopitaux-Facultés. See the map of the region

Travel to CIRAD-Lavalette by public transport

From the SNCF railway station, take the tram marked "Mosson", get off at the "Saint Eloi" stop and take the bus "La Navette" from the "Saint Eloi" stop in rue du Docteur Pezet. Get off at the last stop, "Agropolis Lavalette", cross over avenue Agropolis and walk for another 200 m. The journey takes 30 minutes from the station.

Ticket price : Single Ticket :1,10 euros, 10 tickets : 9,30 euros. Tickets can be bought from automatic machines with coins or credit cards.
