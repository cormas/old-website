Download the first models

 

In order to test Cormas, you can download the following models:

  • four didactic models
    • An example of cellular automata [FireAutomata],
    • An example of situated agents [Pursuit],
    • An example of communicating agents [PlotsRental]
    • An example of aggregative spatial entities [TSE]
  • two theoretical models
    • The famous Axtell and Epstein model [SugarScape]
    • Emergence of Resource-Sharing Conventions [Dricol]
  • one applied model
    • Hunting of wild meat in Cameroon [Djemiong]

Follow this link to install those models.

In addition, a library of downloadable models is available from the applications pages. Some of them appear as downloadable zip archives.

You are invited to subscribe to Cormas list to keep yourself informed and to communicate with other Cormas users.
