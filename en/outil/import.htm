To import models into Cormas

The way to install models in Cormas is simple:

  • Create a sub-directory of Cormas and name it Models.
    Example:
    C:\vwnc30\Cormas\Models
  • Unzip the file you have downloaded (file.zip) in this directory.
  • In the menu of Cormas, click on Import.



  • A window pops up. Double-click on the model of your choice.



  • The model is charged ! Thereafter, you will not have to re-import it any more.
