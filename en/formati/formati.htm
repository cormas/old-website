Agent-based modeling & simulation training courses

 

Since 1997, we have delivered training courses on agent-based modeling and simulation with Cormas to tackle issues related to renewable natural resources management. See the list of past editions.

In 2004-2006, we also proposed a set of specialized training sessions in South-East Asia within the framework of the Asia IT&C project.

MISS-ABMS, Multi-platform International Summer School on Agent-Based Modeling & Simulation

In 2011, we have started to introduce other agent-based generic platforms than Cormas, like NetLogo and Gama, to provide a broader perspective on designing, implementing and running agent-based models for renewable resources management. This multi-platform international training course is taking place during two weeks each summer in Montpellier. Supported by experienced trainers, participants can select one of the proposed platforms to develop their own prototype ABM. Check out the next edition.
