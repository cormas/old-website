Diffuse

A simple model of pheromone diffusion by ants

Pierre Bommel

This simple model presents an ant that moves randomly in a radius of 2 space units and deposits pheromones on the ground. The pheromone diffuses on the space and evaporates simultaneously.
This model illustrates how the principle of cellular automata is implemented in Cormas.
